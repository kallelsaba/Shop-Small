About Us


Welcome to "Shop Small", your ultimate guide to exploring the vibrant world of Tunisian small businesses.
Our platform serves as a bridge connecting consumers with local businesses, highlighting their unique stories, products, and services.
Our mission is simple: to showcase the diversity, creativity, and ingenuity of Tunisian entrepreneurs. Whether you're searching for artisanal crafts, savory culinary delights, trendy fashion boutiques, or innovative tech startups, "Shop Small" is your go-to destination.
We believe in the power of community and the impact of supporting local businesses.
By choosing to explore and patronize Tunisian small businesses, you're not just making a purchase, you're contributing to the growth of local economies, fostering innovation, and preserving cultural heritage.


Join us on this journey as we shine a spotlight on the heart and soul of Tunisia's entrepreneurial spirit.
Together, let's celebrate and empower small businesses to thrive and succeed
